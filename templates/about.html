About Ecofoam

A SELF-DRIVEN TEAM

As a group of twelve inspired teenagers, we find ourselves enveloped in the goal of creating a sustainable future free of the plastic from packaging industries that warps us and our mother Earth.

Nitya Arora

Nitya is a student at Singapore American School with a passion for producing solutions that work towards environmental sustainability. As President of Plastofoam, and with experience in molding, designing, and creating technology solutions, Nitya brings direction to the reinvention of Plastofoam's customized products.

Tanisha Mehta

Tanisha is a student at Singapore American School serving as the Vice President of Production of Plastofoam. Ready to set up lab in her own home, Tanisha offers experimental advice and direction to the company's production cycle, bringing you ideas and sustainability in product choice—from recycled coasters to recycled earrings—like never before!

Sarina Ke

Vice President of Public Relations, Sarina Ke serves to globalize our interests to corporations internationally. At Plastofoam, Sarina helps us collaborate with companies in retail to spread awareness about the importance of upcycling styrofoam because planetary cleanliness is our vision. On the side, Sarina can be found reading deeply into history books, or tinkering with mechanics in the laboratory at Singapore American School.

Anushka Parmanand

Anushka Paramanand, a student at Sigapore American School, invests herself in maximizing the consumer benefits of our products. Working to manage the finances of Plastofoam day-in and day-out, Anushka ensures that every cent spent by our consumers is well-delivered to as Vice President of Finances.
